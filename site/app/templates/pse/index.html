Personal search engine

Search rules :
  • If you use the plus sign '+' in front of any word this will force the PSE to show only documents that match all the words Ex: "history +biology", will return less results than "history biology". It does not matter wich word have the plus sign.
  • If you want to exclude documents that contain specific words use the minus sign '-' for Ex.: "history -biology"
  • If you use quotes the PSE will strip them out for its own search, but they will be passed to Google search. Also all plus '+' signs will be removed for Google.
